Well, the Coronavirus or more specifically COVID-19 disease is caused by a novel strain of virus called the COVID-19 virus. The outbreak of this disease was in Wuhan city of China.

Respiratory System

Once the virus has gained access to your body, it affects the respiratory system the most. The symptoms of Corona disease include dry cough, sore throat, shortness of breath

Immunne system

The virus attacks vehemently if the immune system of a person is compromised. The symptoms manifest very quickly, that if not taken care of properly prove to be fatal. So the best possible way to deal with this disease is to prevent it from occurring.
